BTS

BTS (Korean: 방탄소년단; Bangtan Sonyeondan; lit. Bulletproof Boy Scouts), also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010. The band consists of Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook, who co-write or co-produce much of their material. They debuted on June 12th, 2013. Originally a hip hop group, their musical style has evolved to incorporate a wide range of genres, while their lyrics have focused on subjects, the troubles of school-age youth and coming of age, the journey towards self-love, individualism, and the consequences of fame and recognition. Their discography and hard work has also referenced literature, philosophy and psychological concepts, and includes an alternate universe storyline. They are the most popular band and have the most fans in the world than any other artist. Their leader is RM. Their fans are called ARMY.

JIN

Kim Seok-jin, also known professionally as Jin, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and member of the South Korean boy band BTS. Jin was born on December 4, 1992. Jin has co-written and released three solos with BTS: "Awake" in 2016, "Epiphany" in 2018, and "Moon" in 2020, all of which have charted on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart. In 2019, Jin released his first independent song, the digital track "Tonight". He made his official debut as a solo artist in October 2022, with the release of the single "The Astronaut". He is the oldest member in BTS. Many ARMYS call him WWH (World Wide Handsome).

Suga

Min Yoon-gi, known professionally by his stage names Suga (stylized in all caps) and Agust D,[A] is a South Korean rapper, songwriter and record producer. He was born on March 9, 1993. Under Big Hit Music, he debuted as a member of the South Korean boy band BTS in 2013. His first solo mixtape, Agust D, was released in 2016 and re-released in 2018 to digital download and streaming platforms, reaching number three on Billboard's World Albums Chart. In 2020, he released his second solo mixtape, D-2. Suga has songwriting and production credits on over 100 songs. Suga is the 2nd oldest member in BTS. He is known as the fastest rapper.

J-hope

Jung Hoseok also know as J-hope is the 3rd oldest member of BTS. He was the third member to join the band as a trainee after RM and Suga. J-hope is known as the best dancer in BTS. He has 3 solos: Intro: Boy Meets Evil, Mama, and Trivia 起: Just Dance. Before debuting with BTS, J-Hope was part of an underground dance team called Neuron, and took dance classes at Gwangju Music Academy for six years, from fourth grade to his first year in high school when he signed with Big Hit Entertainment. He was relatively well known for his dance skills, and won various local prizes, including first place in a national dance competition in 2008.

RM

Kim Nam-joon, known professionally as RM (formerly Rap Monster), is a South Korean rapper, singer-songwriter and record producer. He is the leader of South Korean boy band BTS. He was born on September 12, 1994. He is the 4th oldest and youngest member of BTS. He is part of the hyung line(the oldest members). He was introduced to the music of American rapper Eminem by his school teacher, which first sparked RM's interest in lyricism. He would print out lyrics that he felt were "cool" and shared them with friends. RM began songwriting at that time. He was so succesful just like the other members. He is the leader of BTS.

Jimin

Park Ji-min, known mononymously as Jimin, is a South Korean singer and dancer. He was born on October 13, 1995. In 2013, he made his debut as a member of the South Korean boy band BTS, under the record label Big Hit Entertainment. Jimin has released three solo tracks. He will be relaesing more soon. Under the band's name he has 3 solos. He has songs with other artists. Jimin debuted as a vocalist and dancer in BTS on June 13, 2013. Jimin is the 3rd youngest member of BTS. He is in the Makne line (youngest members).

V

Kim Tae-hyung, also known professionally as V, is a South Korean singer and member of the boy band BTS. He was born on December 30, 1995. Since his debut with the group in 2013, V has released three solo tracks under their name—"Stigma" in 2016, "Singularity" in 2018, and "Inner Child" in 2020—all of which charted on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart. He appeared on the soundtrack for the television series Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth in 2016 and released his first independent song, the self-composed "Scenery", in 2019. He is the 2nd youngest member of BTS so he is also part of the Makne line (youngest members).

Jungkook

Jeon Jungkook also known as Jungkook is the youngest member of BTS. He was born on September 1, 1997. He has three solo tracks. And he has colabbarated with other artists just like all the other members. He has made one song for the World Cup (FIFA). It is called Dreamers. When he was younger he became a trainee. He then transferred to Singu Middle School in Seoul. In 2011, Jungkook auditioned for the South Korean talent show Superstar K during its auditions in Daegu. Though he was not selected, he received casting offers from seven entertainment companies.
