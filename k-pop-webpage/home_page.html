BTS

BTS is a South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. Consisting of members Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. BTS is a very popular band with many awards. BTS has won 5 Grammys. BTS debuted in 2013. BTS fandom name is "ARMY".

TWICE

Twice is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment. The group is composed of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Twice debuted in 2015. Twice's fandom name is "ONCE".

ITZY

Itzy is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment and consisting of members Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Yuna. They debuted on February 12, 2019, with the release of their single album It'z Different. Itzy's fandom name is "MIDZY".

STRAY KIDS

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment through the 2017 reality show of the same name. The group is composed of eight members: "Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N." Stray Kids debuted on March 2018. Stray Kids fandom name is "STAY".

IU

Lee Ji-eun, also known by her stage name IU, is a South Korean singer-songwriter, record producer and actress. IU is a k-pop soloist. She signed with LOEN Entertainment in 2007 as a trainee and debuted as a singer at the age of fifteen with her first mini album Lost and Found.
