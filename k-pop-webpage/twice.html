TWICE

Twice is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment. The group is composed of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Twice was formed under the television program Sixteen and debuted on October 20, 2015, with the extended play The Story Begins. Twice is currently the 3rd most popular k-pop band and 2nd most famous girl group. On YouTube, BTS, Blackpink and soloist Psy are the only K-pop acts who have music videos with more views than those of Twice. Their debut song was "Like Ooh-Ahh". Twice's most popular song is "TT". Their most viewed MV is "What is Love?". While Twice has always had a significant international following, 2019 has seen the group make major strides toward establishing itself as a global K-pop leader. Twice's songs are known to be a cute concept.

Nayeon

Im Na-yeon, known mononymously as Nayeon, is a South Korean singer. She became a member of the South Korean girl group Twice, under JYP Entertainment, in 2015 as a winning contestant of the reality survival television show Sixteen. Nayeon is the oldest member in Twice. She's one of the most popular members in Korea. Both Nayeon and Jisoo can understand English well, even though they can't speak fluently.She is close friends with BLACKPINK's Jisoo and Jennie since predebut. Her favorite colors are beige, sky blue, and khaki.

Jeongyeon

Jeongyeon is the 2nd oldest member in TWICE. She auditioned for JYP when she was young, but failed to become a trainee. She tried again at JYP Entertainment’s 6th Open Audition on March 1, 2010 and this time succeeded. Jeongyeon was accepted by both SM and JYP Entertainment on the same day, but chose to join JYP. Jeongyeon trained to become a K-pop idol for five years. She officially became a Twice member on October 20, 2015. Her favourite food is: Tteokbokki, meat, and golbangee muchim. She is the younger sister of the actress Gong Seung-yeon. Her sister appeared in the show Sixteen meeting with Jeongyeon and giving her advices.

Momo

Momo is the 3rd oldest member of TWICE She made an appearance in a Girls' Generation documentary when she was 15. She is allergic to dogs. When Momo touches dog fur, she has reactions of itchy skin. Momo wrote the lyrics for the song ''Shot Thru The Heart'' along with Mina and Sana for their Summer Nights special album. Momo has mentioned many times that she always wanted to be a k-pop singer. Momo was also in sixteen just like all the other memebers.

Sana

Sana is the 4th oldest member of TWICE. She is an only child. She is very optimistic. She has anemia. She is very close with GFRIEND's Eunha. Sana is Japanese. She can speak Korean and Japanese. During sixteen (survival TV show) days she was called 4D. Sana is one of the vocalists in the highly successful K-Pop group TWICE, known for her adorable charms and pretty visuals.

Jihyo

Jihyo is the leader of Twice. She was originally set to debut in the girl group 6mix, along with Nayeon and Jeongyeon. Sana was later added to the group. She legally changed her name from Ji-soo to Ji-hyo before Sixteen. She wrote the lyrics for "24/7" with Nayeon for the Twicetagram album. Even as of 2022, she is the only member to had competed in King of Masked Singer. Being the main vocalist, she has had several collaborations and special stages showcasing her abilities.

Mina

Mina is a Japanese singer in a South Korean girl group, Twice. She was born in San Antonio, Texas, United States, and moved to Kobe, Japan, when she was a toddler. Mina learned ballet for 11 years. She's a really quiet person in public. Mina is foodie. She appeared in Junho's “Feel” (Japanese) MV, GOT7's “Stop Stop It”, Wooyoung’s “Rose” (Japanese) MV, and miss A' s “Only You” MV.

Dahyun

Dahyun was born in Seongnam, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. In middle school, she performed a solo in a youth dance festival and was scouted by JYP Entertainment. Dahyun auditioned for SM, JYP, and YG as the same time. She got accepted by all 3 companies, but she chose JYP. Pre-debut she was very popular due to her Eagle dance video which went viral in South Korea. Her nickname is "Tofu". Dahyun has the ability to find the cameras no matter where they are, so it's really hard to film her without Dahyun to notice it.

Chaeyoung

Chaeyoung was born in Seoul, South Korea. She likes disney cartoons and basketball. Chaeyoung likes healthy foods like veggies but doesn't like sweets. She says this is because she grew up with her grandmother when she was young. She is good at making certain sounds like wild-duck-quacking sound or window-wiping sound. Chaeyoung is close to Jinsol from April, because they was in the same class since high school. Her and Somi (I.O.I) are best friends since they were trainees.

Tzuyu

Tzuyu was born in Tainan, Taiwan. She comes from a wealthy family, her parents own a chain of dermatology clinics in southern Taiwan, her mom also owns 2 coffee shops. Tzuyu was not meant to appear on Sixteen but she replaced Lena. (Former JYP trainee). She is the first female K-Pop idol who is from Taiwan debuted as a K-Pop Idol. She is close to Rose from BLACKPINK. She ranked 3rd in the “100 Most Beautiful Faces of 2017”. She is the youngest member of Twice.
