ITZY

ITZY (있지) is a 5-member girl group under JYP Entertainment. They consist of: Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong and Yuna. They debuted on February 11, 2019 with their first single album “IT’z Different”, and the Title Track “Dalla Dalla”. Known for their impressive choreography and catchy lyrics fueled with girl power, ITZY is really *that* group. Keep reading to uncover everything we know about the K-pop girl band.

Yeji

Yeji’s hometown is Jeonju, South Korea. She attended Jeonju Hwasan Elementary School (graduated), Jeonju Geunyoung Middle School (graduated) & Gyogyo Jeonju Commercial Information Reporting School (graduated). She became a trainee in 2016, so she trained for 3 years. She was a contestant on SBS The Fan (Eliminated in EP5) (2018). Yeji auditioned to JYP with “Like Ooh-Ahh” by Twice. She appeared in Stray Kids‘ Survival Show EP1 (2017).

Lia

Lia’s hometown is Incheon, South Korea. Lia was a trainee for more than 2 years. She is a former SM Ent. trainee. Fans say she looks like a mix of BLACKPINK‘s Jisoo and Red Velvet‘s Irene. Lia was born on the same day as Everglow‘s Aisha. Lia is most comfortable with Chaeryeong. Her nicknames are Honey Lia, Cinnamon Lia and Ariana Grande. She was vocally trained at Dream Vocal Academy.

Ryujin

Ryujin’s hometown is Seoul, South Korea. She starred in BTS‘ Love Yourself Highlight Reel (She was J-Hope and Jimin‘s pair). Yang Hyun Suk offered her to join YG, but she decided to stay at JYP. Ryujin was a trainee for 4 years. She was an actress in the movie “The King” (2017). She appeared on Stray Kids’ Survival Show EP1 (2017). Ryujin got casted by JYP at a GOT7 fanmeeting.

Chaeryeong

Chaeryeong's hometown is Yongin, South Korea. She’s IZ*ONE‘s Chaeyeon‘s sister. She also has a younger sister named Chaemin. She became a trainee in 2014. She trained for 5 years. Chaeryeong, along with her sister Chaeyeon, auditioned for Fantagio in 2012, but didn’t make it. She often gets scared easily. She was a contestant on SIXTEEN when she was 14 (Ranked #12). She is close friends with all the members of TWICE, Jeon Somi and Fromis_9‘s Jiwon.

Yuna

Yuna’s hometown is Suwon, South Korea. She attended Suwon Hwayang Elementary School (graduated), Yeongbok Girls’ Middle School (graduated) & Hanlim Entertainment Arts High School (Practical Dance Department / student). She appeared in BTS‘ Highlight Reels (She was Jungkook‘s pair) (2017).Fans say she looks like PRISTIN‘s Kyulkyung. Yuna is a extrovert. She is the tallest member in ITZY. She is also the youngest member of Itzy.
