STRAY KIDS

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment. The group consists of 8 members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. They debuted on March 25, 2018. The group was created through the survival program with the same name, Stray Kids. As of February 10th, 2022, they are also signed under Republic Records. Fans of Stray Kids are called "Stays".

Bang Chan

Bang Chan was born in South Korea, but he moved to Sydney, Australia when he was very young. His nicknames given by his members: Kangaroo and Koala. He speaks English, Korean, Japanese, and a bit of Chinese. He can play the guitar and the piano. Bang Chan is close friends with GOT7‘s BamBam and Yugyeom and ENHYPEN‘s Jake.

Lee Know

Born in Gimpo, South Korea. He is an only child. He has a unique and fun personality, a 4D personality. He has a fear of heights. Lee Know really likes to read and his favorite author is currently Keigo Higashino. If he wasn’t in Stray Kids, he would be a dancer. He started dancing since he was in middle school. He was a backup dancer for BTS during their Japan tour.

Changbin

Born in Yongin, South Korea. His nickname given by his members: Mogi (mosquito), Jingjingie (whiny), Teokjaengie (chin) and Binnie. His specialties are writing lyrics and rap. If he wasn’t in Stray Kids, he would be a producer, writer or maybe tattoo master. He is in pre-debut group/sub-unit 3RACHA with Chan and Jisung. His stage name in 3RACHA is SPEARB.

Hyunjin

He was born in Seoul, South Korea. Hyunjin is an only child. His nicknames are Jinnie and The Prince. Hyunjin’s English name is Sam Hwang. If he wasn’t in Stray Kids, he would be an interior designer. He talks in his sleep and is the hardest to wake up. He trained for 2 years. He can talk in basic english but not advanced. His hobbies are dancing, reading books and playing sports.

Han

Born in Incheon, South Korea. His nickname given by his members: Squirrel. He speaks English. His hobby is to eat cheesecake while watching a movie. His special ability is a voice impression of Doraemon. Han has trypophobia (a fear of clusters of small holes). If he wasn’t in Stray Kids, he would be a producer.

Felix

His parents are Korean, but he was born in Seven Hills, a suburb of Sydney, Australia. His nicknames given his members: Bbijikseu, Bbajikseu, Bbujikseu, and Jikseu. Hobbies: Listening to music, dancing, shopping (especially clothes), traveling, and beatboxing. Felix likes soccer. He’s close friends with ENHYPEN‘s Jake. If he wasn’t in Stray Kids, he would be a songwriter.

Seungmin

Born in Seoul, South Korea. Has an older sister. He graduated from Cheongdam High School. His nickname given by his members: Snail; his nickname given by fans: Sunshine. If he wasn’t in Stray Kids, he would be a photographer or a prosecutor. Seungmin is the 2nd youngest member and 7th oldest member of Stray Kids. He is friends with AB6IX‘ Daehwi since high school.

I.N.

He was born in Busan, South Korea. His nicknames given by his members: Desert Fox, Our Maknae, Spoon Worm Yang, Fiona and Bean Worm. His special ability is singing trot. He is the youngest member of Stray kids. He is the Maknae. If he wasn’t in Stray Kids, he would be a singer, or an elementary school teacher since he likes kids. I.N. use to be a child model when he was seven. He trained for 2 years to be a k-pop idol.
